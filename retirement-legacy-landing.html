The Real Problem

Doing "okay" on paper doesn't always mean you're on track

Some of the most common gaps I see, across CPF, insurance and investments:

⚠

CPF alone may not stretch as far as you'd like once you factor in inflation and a longer retirement.

⚠

Insurance, CPF and investments were set up at different times, by different people, with no single plan tying them together.

⚠

No will or LPA in place — so decisions during a crisis, or after, default to the law rather than your wishes.

⚠

Too busy — or unsure where to start — to sit down and actually map out the next 10 to 20 years.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Who do you usually work with?

Mostly young professionals starting their financial plan, mid-career adults navigating CPF and retirement decisions, and women building financial independence — though the framework applies more broadly too.

What happens during the complimentary session?

We'll look at your current CPF, insurance, investments and estate documents together, and I'll walk you through where the gaps are and what a fuller plan could look like.

Do I need a minimum amount of savings or investments?

Not for the initial conversation — it's more important that you're ready to build an actual plan rather than continuing to piece things together on your own.

Is this financial advice?

The session is a planning conversation to understand your situation. Any specific recommendation follows a proper needs analysis, in line with regulatory requirements.
