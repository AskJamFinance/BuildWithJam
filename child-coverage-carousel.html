Key Idea · 核心概念

A child does not need income replacement, but the family may need financial support during treatment and caregiving.

孩子不需要收入替代，但在治疗与照顾期间，家庭可能需要额外的经济支援。

What Affects the Amount · 影响因素

What Affects the
Coverage Amount?

什么因素会影响保障金额？

There isn't a one-size-fits-all amount — it depends on your family's situation.
保障金额没有标准答案，取决于每个家庭的需要。

👨‍👩‍👧
One parent may stop working若其中一位父母需要暂停工作

A larger payout can help replace the temporary loss of income. 较高的保障可弥补因照顾孩子而减少的收入。

🏥
Prefer private healthcare偏向选择私人医疗

Private medical treatment may cost significantly more. 私人医疗费用通常较高，因此需要较高保障。

✈️
Overseas treatment options希望拥有海外治疗选择

Additional funds provide flexibility for specialised or overseas treatment. 额外保障可为海外或专科治疗提供更多选择。

💰
Dual income family家庭主要依赖双薪收入

If one parent stops working, household income is affected more. 若其中一位父母暂停工作，家庭收入可能受到较大影响。

Key Takeaway · 重点提醒

The right amount isn't the highest amount.

It's the amount that lets your family focus on your child's recovery without unnecessary financial stress.
最适合的保障，不一定是最高的保障，而是在孩子需要治疗时，让家庭能够专注照顾，而不是担心经济压力。

Final Thought · 最后提醒

Coverage Is
About Choices

保障的意义，是保留选择

The goal is not to insure a child for the highest amount. The goal is to ensure that an illness does not become a financial crisis for the family.

重点不是为孩子购买最高的保障金额，而是确保疾病不会演变成家庭的财务危机。

Review the child's coverage together with the parents' own protection — family financial security still depends heavily on the parents' income.
同时也要检视父母本身的保障，因为家庭财务稳定仍然高度依赖父母的收入。
